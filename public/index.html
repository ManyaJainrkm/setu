What Setu is — and what it isn't

We believe unbiased support starts with honesty about ourselves:

  • Setu's guides are AI personas, each designed around an established counselling approach. They are not real, licensed clinicians.
  • Setu is not therapy and not a replacement for professional mental health or medical care. It's a place to think out loud, feel heard, and find a next step.
  • Setu will never diagnose you, advise on medication, or push you toward any decision, product, or belief.
  • If you're in crisis, Setu will always point you to real human help, immediately.
  • Your privacy is structural, not a promise: Setu is a static site that runs on your own Anthropic API key. Your messages go straight from your browser to Anthropic, and your conversations live only in your browser — there's no Setu server in the middle to store anything.

That's the deal. No fine print.

In crisis right now? Please reach out to real humans immediately — India: Tele-MANAS 14416 · US: call/text 988 · elsewhere: findahelpline.com or your local emergency services.
