Your Anthropic API key

Setu is free and runs on your Anthropic key — that's what keeps it unbiased and lets us host it with no accounts and no data on our side.

  • Your key is stored only in this browser (localStorage) and is sent straight to Anthropic, never to us.
  • Messages use your Anthropic credit, typically a fraction of a cent each.
  • Don't have one? Create a key at console.anthropic.com (you'll need to add a little credit).
